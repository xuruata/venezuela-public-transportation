Operational segment (Las Adjuntas → Ayacucho) opened in stages: Las Adjuntas to
Alí Primera in November 2006; extension to Ayacucho in October 2015. Line 2
(Independencia, Guaicaipuro, San Antonio de los Altos) has been under
construction with intermittent reports of station completion.
