## Completeness caveat
**This system is the least complete in the dataset.** Wikipedia describes line corridors
without enumerating every stop, and OpenStreetMap coverage of Barquisimeto BRT stops is
sparse (only 1 stop tagged with Transbarca network as of 2026-05-25). The stop list here
includes the named major stops described in Wikipedia plus terminuses.

Contributions welcome to:
- Enumerate every L1 and L2 stop in order (W→E).
- Add coordinates from on-the-ground knowledge or surveys.
- Document the 6 feeder routes (101, 201, 601, 801, 802, 901).
