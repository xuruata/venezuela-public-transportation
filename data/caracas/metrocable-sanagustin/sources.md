## Coordinate caveat
Stop coordinates are approximate (±100 m) — derived from descriptions of route alignment
along the San Agustín hillside, not from official surveys. The system is short enough
(1.8 km) that the stations form a roughly linear chain south from Parque Central station.

## Operational status
As of 2020 reporting, only 3 of 5 stations (Parque Central, Hornos de Cal, La Ceiba) are
operational. El Manguito and San Agustín are marked closed pending maintenance. Update
needed if these reopen.
